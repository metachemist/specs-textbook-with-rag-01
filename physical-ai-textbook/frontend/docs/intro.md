# Introduction

Welcome to the Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics Textbook. This interactive textbook combines traditional learning materials with AI-powered features to enhance your understanding of physical AI concepts.

## What You'll Learn

In this textbook, you'll explore:

- **Module 1**: ROS 2 - The Robot Operating System
- **Module 2**: Digital Twin - Simulation and Modeling
- **Module 3**: AI-Robot Brain - Perception and Decision Making
- **Module 4**: Vision-Language-Action - Advanced Robotics Applications

## How to Use This Textbook

This textbook features several AI-powered tools:

- **RAG Chatbot**: Ask questions about the content and get AI-powered answers
- **Personalization**: Content adapts based on your hardware setup
- **Localization**: Content available in multiple languages

Use the sidebar to navigate through the modules, and don't hesitate to use the AI chat widget to ask questions about the content!
